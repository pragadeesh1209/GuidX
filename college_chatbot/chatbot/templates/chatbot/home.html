About
About This Project

GuidX is an AI-powered career guidance chatbot built for students across India who have completed their 12th grade and are deciding their next educational step.

The system uses the Google Gemini API (gemini-2.5-flash) to generate intelligent, personalized responses to any career or course question — in English, Tamil, Hindi, Malayalam, or Telugu.

Unlike static career websites, GuidX understands natural language, remembers your marks and interests within the conversation, and gives real-time personalized guidance covering colleges across all of India.

Built with Django and Python. Voice input and output supported. All conversations saved securely to your account.
